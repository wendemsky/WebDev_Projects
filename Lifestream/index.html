Donate blood, you have nothing to lose.

BLOOD, THE MOST ESSENTIAL PART OF ONE'S LIFE, IS THE MOST UNIQUE ELEMENT. IT CAN BE DONATED, RECEIVED AND CAN SAVE SO MANY LIVES.

Blood donations are used for patients in need of surgery, cancer treatment and transfusions for blood loss from traumatic injuries, according to red cross, one donation can save up to three lives. It doesn't only have benefits for the recipient but also the donor. India needs at least 15 million blood donors every year but only 10 million units are donated. We need to create awareness about this.

A-POSITIVE

Contains type-A antigens. Antigens are markers on the surface of a blood cell. This is one of the most common blood types.

A-NEGATIVE

Can be given to A and AB patients. Important blood products can be used for many patients needing transfusions.

B-POSITIVE

Constitutes 9% of the population. Contains both B and Rh antigens.Can be given to both the B+ and AB+ patients.

B-NEGATIVE

Constitutes 2% of the population. B- blood type contains the B antigen only. B- type red blood cells can be given to any of the B and AB patients.

AB-POSITIVE

Constitutes 4% of the population. Is a very rare and important blood type. The plasma can be given to all patients.

AB-NEGATIVE

Constitutes 1% of the population. The rarest blood type, and is in high demand. The plasma can be given to all patients.

O-POSITIVE

Is a very important universal red blood cell type. Can be used in emergency situations such as traumatic bleeding.

O-NEGATIVE

Is the universal blood type as it has neither A, B or Rh antigens. It can be used for any patient, no matter their blood type.

DONATING BLOOD IS AN ACT OF HEAVEN. WE PROVIDE BLOOD DONATION CAMPS FOR THE PEOPLE WHO WANT TO DONATE THEIR PRECIOUS BLOOD

Below are the listed blood donation camps

A-POSITIVE DONATION

A- positive blood is needed in vast quantities as majority of the people have this type of blood group. Your blood can be given to A+ and AB+.

B-POSITIVE DONATION

B- positive blood is a major constituent of the world. Donation of B-positive blood is essential. Your blood can be given to B+ and AB+.

A-NEGATIVE DONATION

A- negative is a very rare type of blood. Donation of A-negative is critical, can be used to save many lives. Your blood can be donated to A-, A+, AB-, AB+.

B-NEGATIVE DONATION

B- negative is a very rare type of blood. Can be donated to any of the blood groups. Donation of B- blood is very essential indeed.

AB-NEGATIVE DONATION

Constitutes about 1% of the population. It is extremely rare. It can be donated to all the groups, but can receive from negative groups only.

AB-POSITIVE DONATION

AB-positive is one of the most founded blood groups of people in the world. AB-positive blood is very important as it can be donated to any positive blood group.

O-POSITIVE DONATION

O-positive is the most abundant blood type. 38% of the world constitutes of them. These people can donate blood to all the positive groups in the world.

O-NEGATIVE DONATION

O-negative is a rare type of blood. Only a few people donate it. These people can donate their blood to all groups, but can recieve from O- only.
